Akachukwu Emeka, (b.1963)

Akachukwu Benjamin Emeka is an alumnus of the Fine and Applied art program at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka. He holds a post-graduate diploma in Gender studies from the University of Abuja. A painter and a poet, Akachukwu sees his work in two mediums, which often share common themes, as both a window into the universe of the creative mind and a casing to ponder in its own right. Akachukwu draws motivation for his work from the profound excellence of the points of interest in his lived environment. ... Points of interest such as cloud arrangements, individuals hurrying from work, creatures and winged animals, and the spirits that monitor all activity. His most recent project, entitled Forms from my Sky, employs the use of vat dye and Acrylic on canvas
